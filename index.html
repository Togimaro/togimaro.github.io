C# NativeAOT XNA/FNA/MonoGame C++ SDL3 DirectX 12 Vulkan AGC GNM
2025 - Dotemu
Xbox One (GDK), Windows Store (GDK), PS4, PS5, Nintendo Switch

  • Ported FNA/SDL3 to PS4, including a new graphical backend
  • Level optimization to target 60fps on Nintendo Switch at full resolution
  • Implemented level of detail to reduce overdraw and texture atlasing to optimize batching
  • Replaced the Vulkan backend of SDL3 with NVN to gain perfomance on Nintendo Switch
  • Implemented online-crossplay with console requirements in mind
  • Worked on MARVEL Cosmic Invasion (also by Dotemu) in parallel, implemented a consistant build pipeline between productions
